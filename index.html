How To Use

  1. Keep all .ttf files and font.css together in your project.
  2. Load font.css in your page head.
  3. Use font-family: 'Death Ledger', serif; on any element.
<link rel="stylesheet" href="./font.css" />
h1 { font-family: 'Death Ledger', serif; font-weight: 700; }
<h1>The names written here will die</h1>
